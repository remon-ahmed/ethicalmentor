Q. 12  /  100

With reference to the Constitution of India, consider the following statements about the 'Right to Constitutional Remedies':

1. It can be suspended during a National Emergency.
2. Only the Supreme Court can issue writs under this article.

Which of the statements given above is/are correct?
A 1 only
B 2 only
C Both 1 and 2
D Neither 1 nor 2
✅ Answer: C — Both 1 and 2
Article 32 (Right to Constitutional Remedies) can be suspended under Article 359 during a National Emergency. While Article 32 empowers the Supreme Court to issue writs, High Courts also issue writs under Article 226 for fundamental rights enforcement. Hence, statement 2 is also correct.
